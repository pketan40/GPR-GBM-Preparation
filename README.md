# Godrej Park Retreat - First General Body Meeting (GBM)

**Date:** 21st March 2026  
**Venue:** Godrej Park Retreat, Sarjapur Road, Bengaluru  
**Organized by:** GPR Residents Welfare Association (RWA)

---

## About

This repository contains the official presentation and summary report for the **First General Body Meeting (GBM)** of Godrej Park Retreat Owners Association. The documents cover 8 months of RWA operations since the handover in July 2025.

## Files

| File | Description |
|------|-------------|
| [GPR_GBM_Presentation_v5.pptx](./GPR_GBM_Presentation_v5.pptx) | 30-slide presentation with green resort theme, partner logos, and GPR images |
| [GPR_GBM_Summary_Report.docx](./GPR_GBM_Summary_Report.docx) | Detailed summary report covering all agenda points |

## GBM Agenda (8 Points)

1. **Welcome & Introduction** - RWA Formation, Board Members, Tower Representatives
2. **Vendor Partnerships** - 11 partnerships (Sparsh Hospital, Brother Chemist, MyGate, Tilt.bike, AdOnMo, HDFC Bank, Airtel, Laundry & Vegetable vendors, Apollo Clinic, Park+ ANPR)
3. **Financial Statement** - CAM Collection, Expenses, Net Position from July 2025 onwards
4. **Welfare & Infrastructure** - Security, Housekeeping, Lifts, Water Quality, Green Initiatives
5. **Clubhouse & Amenities** - 6-month construction plan, future amenities
6. **Governance Framework** - Sub-committees, vendor policy, financial controls
7. **Future Roadmap** - Upcoming projects, revenue streams, community programs
8. **Q&A and Closing** - Open floor for residents
